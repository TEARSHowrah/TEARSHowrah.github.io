TEARS

"TEARS” is an organization dedicated to welfare of street children since year 2000 in Uttarpara station by Miss GITA ROUTH simply collecting them near to the yard & trying to educating them.She is from a poor family either & could manage to spend 15 or 20 INR to feed them. She had nothing except courage & wisdom.Later on she met Mr.KISHOR JAISWAL (Social worker & president BVNS). TEARS FORMED.They are studying further. In Howrah she is taking care of 40 children by providing them food, education & clothing.

PROBLEM OF TEARS Tears can give them shelter for the day but not in the night as per the provision of JJ Act. In the day time they are behaving well but in the night they are returning to the Howrah station & all the hard work during the day time putted in going in vein. We need to build a home for these unfortunate children of God, to keep & protect them. There shall be kitchen, bed & school inside so that a decent life can be gifted to them. We have the required land as donation; we need the money for erection of the dream home for them.

THE PLAN TEARS run on donation. Its monthly expenditure now is 22500 INR p.m..
