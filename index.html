WE care about others

We are humans

It is important to remember that most of these children lack of respectful interactions with other humans.The most important thing is to treat them with respect as you would to any other person and show them some decency.

These children feel invisible and abandoned by their government and the adults who were supposed to care for them. Don’t expect anything in return, though, “What is very important is for the giver to give with a kind heart and clear vision,”

We give children the care, education and development they deserve. Children living, begging or working on the street, ensure that all children on the streets are not ignored but get the help they deserve.
